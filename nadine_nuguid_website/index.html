I am a Filipino-Canadian artist based in the GTA who creates both illustrative and graphic artwork. I am currently available to take on new projects and commissions.

Why hire me?

I started with Illustration with sights of doing both Editorial and Sequential art. Right after, I finished my degree I continued my education in Interactive Media Design as I felt that I could grow more in design.

With skills in both creativity and design, I can bring your ideas to life and tell a story through my illustration and designs. I am committed to creating something that is long-lasting, memorable and timeless. My work explores the movement and energy that life offers through a sketchy style with bright and bold colours. I want to work with people who want to make things from the heart and have something they want to share to the world.
